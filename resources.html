JEF-ILO Programme Resources

Collaborative initiatives supporting Jamaica's employment landscape

Understanding The Sexual Harassment Act 2021

Workshop materials and comprehensive guidance to help employers understand and implement the Sexual Harassment Act 2021, ensuring workplace safety and compliance across Jamaica's employment sector.

Occupational Safety and Health Bill

JEF consultation submissions on the OSH Bill, which is in final stages of legislative development. Resources to support employers in preparing for new occupational safety and health standards.

HIV and AIDS Workplace Policy

Policy templates developed in collaboration with the Ministry of Health, Ministry of Labour and Social Security, and international bodies. Comprehensive resources for creating inclusive workplace policies.

ILO Just Transition Project 2024

JEF participation in the International Labour Organization's Just Transition initiative, supporting Jamaica's sustainable economic transition while protecting employment and workers' rights.
