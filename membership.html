Why Join JEF?

Advocacy

JEF is the sole Jamaican employer representative to the International Labour Organisation (ILO). We advocate on your behalf in tripartite councils, at the Caribbean Employers' Confederation (19 countries), and at the International Organisation of Employers (IOE).

HR & IR Support

Access expert guidance on labour law, disciplinary procedures, collective bargaining, and grievance handling. JEF's Senior Director — Head of Workplace Solutions provides direct advisory support to members.

Research

Annual Salary & Benefits Survey, Summary of Labour Laws (English + Mandarin), Decade of Arbitration volumes, and labour market analysis to support your business decisions and strategic planning.

Training

Over 1,300 professionals trained in 2024–2025. Courses in HR, IR, Occupational Safety & Health, and Leadership. Online and face-to-face options. Certificate of Participation upon completion.

Partnerships & Collaborations

Access to JEF's network of regional and international employer organisations, UWI Labour Studies Institute, ILO programmes, and JEF Online digital learning platform.

Economic Benefits

Exclusive member pricing on training courses, surveys, consultancy services, and events. Members benefit from JEF's active lobbying for a favourable business environment.

Member Activities & Events

Stay connected with JEF throughout the year. Our calendar of activities provides networking, learning, and advocacy opportunities.

Executive Meetings

Frequency: Bi-monthly

Annual General Meeting

Timing: Within 3 months after end of Financial Year (typically May)

JEF CEO Breakfast Series

Frequency: Quarterly — exclusive networking and knowledge-sharing events for senior leadership

In-Chambers (Labour Laws Forum)

Frequency: Quarterly — deep-dive sessions on labour law updates and compliance

Members Forum

Frequency: Periodic — open forums for discussion on industry issues and member feedback

JEF Annual Convention

Timing: May each year — our flagship annual gathering of employers, leaders, and stakeholders
