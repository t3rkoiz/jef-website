Our Departments

Membership Services

Dedicated to member liaison, marketing events, major conferences and workshops. We foster relationships with our 300+ members and create opportunities for industry engagement and professional development.

Workplace Solutions

Specializing in Industrial Relations advisory, training and consultancy services. We help employers navigate complex workplace challenges and foster productive labor-management relations.

Finance & Accounts

Responsible for comprehensive financial management and accounting operations. We ensure fiscal responsibility and transparency in all JEF operations and member-related transactions.

Administration

Managing facility operations and general administration. We provide the operational backbone that enables our teams to deliver exceptional service to JEF members.

The Federation's success rests heavily on the commitment of the dedicated and hardworking team."
