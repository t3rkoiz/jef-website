Our Mission

To lead the development of an enabling business environment through the effective representation and provision of value-added services that strengthens the ability of employers to optimise competitiveness and workplace harmony.

Our Vision

To remain the premier employers' organisation in the region, by continuously providing value-added services to our members, thereby assisting them in achieving global competitiveness.

Strategic Plan 2024–2029
Our Four Strategic Pillars

JEF's five-year strategic plan centres on four interconnected priorities designed to strengthen Jamaica's employer community and deliver greater value to our members.

01

Service Delivery

Advancing and improving local economic development initiatives, industrial relations advisory, and workplace solutions to empower members with best practices.

02

Human Capital Development

Targeted upskilling, apprenticeships, and partnerships with industry, education, and government to match labour supply with demand in high-growth areas.

03

Operational Efficiency

Leveraging digital tools, AI, and e-commerce platforms to streamline operations and prepare Jamaica's workforce for the Fourth Industrial Revolution.

04

Revenue Enhancement

Diversifying income streams through non-core services, HR, health and pensions products, loyalty programmes, and B2B e-commerce platforms for members.

Key Advocacy Priorities
Improving Productivity Digital Transformation Addressing Skills Gaps Labour Market Reform Green Jobs & Just Transition Unemployment Insurance Reform
