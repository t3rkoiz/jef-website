Services Built for Jamaican Employers

Comprehensive expertise and dedicated support across human resources, occupational safety, events, business development, industrial relations, and research—helping employers thrive in Jamaica's dynamic labour market.

👥

Human Resource & Workplace Solutions

Our IR consultancy, HR policy development, contract review, dispute mediation, and workforce best-practice support is tailored to Jamaican labour law, helping you navigate complex employment relationships with confidence.

We advise on disciplinary procedures, termination processes, redundancy, grievance handling, and staff relations. Our team provides customised HR policy development aligned to the Labour Relations and Industrial Disputes Act, ensuring your workplace practices are both compliant and effective.

Reduce legal risk, resolve disputes faster, and improve employee relations.

Key Benefits

  • Reduced legal and compliance risk
  • Faster dispute resolution
  • Improved employee relations & morale
  • Customised policies aligned to Jamaican labour law
  • Expert guidance on sensitive employment matters

Occupational Safety, Health & Environment

Workplace OSH audits, compliance support, hazard assessments, and safety officer training empower your organisation to meet and exceed Jamaican occupational safety requirements, including the pending OSH Bill.

JEF assists employers in developing comprehensive safety management systems, conducting risk assessments, and delivering training for Safety Officers. We also help develop Emergency Response Plans that protect your workforce and demonstrate your commitment to a safe workplace.

Stay ahead of regulations, protect your workforce, and reduce incidents.

Key Benefits

  • Regulatory compliance & legislative awareness
  • Reduced workplace incidents & injuries
  • Comprehensive safety management systems
  • Trained safety officers & emergency protocols
  • Enhanced employee safety culture

Event Planning & Coordination

End-to-end planning for corporate events, stakeholder engagements, conferences, seminars, and flagship employer events. JEF has a proven track record delivering world-class events that connect employers, government, and labour stakeholders.

For over 40 years, we have hosted the Annual Business & Workplace Convention, CEO Breakfast Series (bringing together 80+ senior executives per session), Members Forum, INN Chambers discussions, and National Employers' Month celebrations. Whether you need a small corporate gathering or a large-scale conference, our experienced team delivers excellence.

World-class events delivered on time, connecting employers with key stakeholders.

Key Benefits

  • End-to-end event management & coordination

Industrial Relations & Policy

Expert IR advisory, representation in collective bargaining, policy development, and employer advocacy at national and regional levels. JEF represents employers on critical tripartite bodies shaping Jamaica's labour landscape.

Our team serves on the Labour Advisory Council (tripartite meetings chaired by the Ministry of Labour), Minimum Wage Commission, Industrial Disputes Tribunal (3 panels), National Council for Persons with Disabilities, and participates in ILO international conferences. We translate complex labour policy into actionable guidance for your organisation.

Expert guidance on collective agreements, disputes, and policy changes.

Key Benefits

  • Expert guidance on labour agreements & disputes
  • Representation in collective bargaining
  • Policy advocacy & influence
  • Early warning on legislative changes
  • Tripartite engagement support

Research & Development

Annual Salary and Benefits Survey, labour market analysis, and the Summary of Labour Laws publication provide employers with data-driven intelligence for compensation, hiring, and strategic planning decisions.

JEF produces the Annual Compensation Survey (the definitive resource for market-rate salary data), maintains the Summary of Labour Laws (available in English and Mandarin for Jamaica's Chinese employer community through the Chinese Benevolent Association), and publishes Volumes of the Decade of Arbitration for training and research reference.

Make data-driven decisions on compensation and stay informed on law changes.

Key Benefits

  • Market-rate salary & benefits data
  • Comprehensive labour law summaries
  • Data-driven compensation planning
  • Research publications & case studies
  • Competitive intelligence & market trends
