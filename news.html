International Representation

JEF actively participates in global employer forums, shaping policy and standards that benefit Jamaica's business community.

🌍
International Labour Organization
Jamaica's sole employers' representative to the ILO, influencing international labour standards and policies.
🤝
Caribbean Employers' Confederation
Member of CEC representing 19 countries. Wayne Chen serves as Immediate Past CEC President.
💼
International Organization of Employers
Global network of business federations co-hosting conventions and collaborative initiatives with JEF.
🏛️
CARICOM Engagement
Active participation in Caribbean Community Heads of Government Meetings and regional policy development.

Established in 1958, JEF is the sole employers' representative to the ILO in Jamaica representing 300+ corporate and individual members.
